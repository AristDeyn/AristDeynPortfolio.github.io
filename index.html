Arist Deyn Portfolio 2026

My project has been to combine the story telling focused minimalism of David Mazzuchelli, Alex Toth, and Darwyn Cooke with the Ashcan Realists. I've also put emphasis on rhythmic pacing (influenced Frank Miller on DKR) and cinematic camera techniques (influenced by Katsuhiro Otomo and Frank Quitely as well as filmmakers like Pier Pasolini, Robert Altman, Dario Argento, and many more)..
